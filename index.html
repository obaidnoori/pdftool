      Privacy Policy

      We are committed to protecting your privacy. Here's exactly how 76 PDF Suite handles your data.

      🔒

      Files Never Leave Your Device

      All PDF processing happens inside your browser using JavaScript. No file is ever uploaded to any server — not ours, not anyone else's.

      🚫

      No Tracking or Analytics

      We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel or any other tracking technology. We do not track which tools you use or how often.

      🍪

      No Cookies

      76 PDF Suite does not set any cookies on your device. Your browsing is completely private and leaves no trace on our end.

      🗑️

      No Data Retention

      Because nothing is uploaded, there is nothing for us to store or delete. Closing the tab removes all traces of your work.

      📡

      Network Connections Blocked

      Our Content Security Policy blocks all outgoing network connections from tool operations, making data leakage technically impossible.

      🛠️

      Open Source Libraries

      We use trusted open-source libraries: pdf-lib, PDF.js, and JSZip. All processing code is publicly auditable.

      Last updated: 2026. This policy applies to all tools on this site. If you have questions, please contact us. 76 PDF Suite is a project by 76 Supplier Company.
